What is Agentic AI?

Published by AIWithViveka

Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can make independent decisions, plan tasks, and execute actions autonomously. These systems combine LLMs, RAG workflows, and agent-based architectures to perform complex tasks that usually require human-like reasoning.
